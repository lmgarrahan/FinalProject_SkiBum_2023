SkiBum

your ride to the slopes

Our Mission

SkiBum is a mobile application that connects like-minded winter adventurers with carpools to Utahs ski resorts. Our mission is to increase accessibility to Utah's world class resorts and reduce emissions affecting Utahs fragile ecosystem

Why we SkiBum

We all know the air quality is Utah has seen better days, do your part to reduce emissions by sharing a ride.

SkiBum is good for your wallet. Split the gas with your fellow carpoolers & reap the benefits of reduced & free parking!

The best part? Meet rad new friends to enjoy powder days with! The mountain is always better with frirends! The more the merrier!

How to SkiBum

Step 1

Create an Account, use your ski pass credentials to more easily find carpools

Step 2

Select a Resort of Choice and preferred travel time.

Step 3

Chose the carpool that works for you! Filter by ratings, available seats and experience level.

Step 4

Head to your meetup location and hit the road!
